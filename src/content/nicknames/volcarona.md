## Origins and data

<div class="room-box">
		<div class="room-box-left">
			<ul>
				<li><strong>Ability</strong>: Flame Body</li>
				<li><strong>Hidden Ability</strong>: Swarm</li>
				<li><strong>Height</strong>: 5'03" or 1.6 m</li>
				<li><strong>Weight</strong>: 101.4 lbs. or 46.0 kg</li>
			</ul>
			<p>Volcarona was introduced in Generation 5 with a dual Bug/Fire typing. Known as the Sun Pokémon, Volcarona is moth-like Pokémon with read wings arranged in a flower petal shape, a furry white thorax, and a sky blue and black abdomen.</p>
			<p>It has ancient and future relatives, being Slither Wing and <a href="/nicknames/iron-moth/">Iron Moth</a> respectively.</p>
