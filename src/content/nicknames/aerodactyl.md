## Origins and data

<div class="room-box">
		<div class="room-box-left">
			<ul>
				<li><strong>Ability</strong>: Rock Head or Pressure (Aerodactyl); Tough Claws (Mega Aerodactyl)</li>
				<li><strong>Hidden Ability</strong>: Unnerve</li>
				<li><strong>Height</strong>: 5'11" (1.8 m) - Aerodactyl;<br>6'11" (2.1 m) - Mega Aerodactyl</li>
				<li><strong>Weight</strong>: 130.1 lbs. (59.0 kg) - Aerodactyl;<br>174.2 lbs. (79.0 kg) - Mega Aerodactyl</li>
			</ul>
			<p>Aerodactyl was introduced in Generation 1 with a dual Rock/Flying typing. Known as the Fossil Tree Pokémon, it's a large, grey pterosaur-like Pokémon with purple membranous wings and a big jaw.</p>
			<p>It also has a mega form, Mega Aerodactyl, introduced in Generation 6 which has green eyes spiky rocks all over its body.</p>
		</div>
