Centiskorch is a Pokémon first introduced in Pokémon Sword & Shield in 2019. It is a Fire/Bug type and evolves from Sizzlipede starting at level 28. Centiskorch can also Gigantamax into Gigantamax Centiskorch. It is known as the Radiator Pokémon.

> When it heats up, its body temperature reaches about 1,500 degrees Fahrenheit. It lashes its body like a whip and launches itself at enemies.

Centiskorch is called the Radiator Pokémon and is said to be inspired by the Chinese red-headed centipede and radiators (hence the names).
