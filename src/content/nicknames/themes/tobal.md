Tobal No. 1 and Tobal 2 are fighting games developed by DreamFactory and published by Square Enix in Japan (Tobal No. 1 was published by Sony in the rest of the world). I just started playing No. 1 and thought the characters would make cool nicknames. You have a rabbit fighter, a massive blue caped fighter, a weird chicken-looking guy, a robot... classic Japanese gaming weirdness.

## Tobal No. 1

* **Chuji** - Scrafty, Lucario, Ceruledge (any humanoid Pokémon really)
* **Gren Kuts** - shiny Scrafty (because of the green), Gallade, any green Pokémon
* **Epon** - Gardevoir, Tsareena, Lopunny, Nidoqueen, Vespiquen, Froslass, Lilligant, Salazzle, Hatterene, [Tinkaton](/nicknames/tinkaton/), [Ogerpon](/nicknames/ogerpon/)
* **Oliems** - Blaziken, Zapdos, Unfezant, Archeops
* **Fei** - Drampa, [Alakazam](/nicknames/alakazam/)
* **Homs** - Magearna, Beheeyem, Iron Valiant
* **Ill Goga** - Giratina, Houndoom, Gliscor
* **Mary Ivonskaya** - same as Epon
* **Mufu** - same as Homs, also [Snorlax](/nicknames/snorlax/), [Bellibolt](/nicknames/bellibolt/)
* **Nork** - Oinkologne, [Emboar](/nicknames/emboar/), Grumpig, Regigigas, [Annihilape](/nicknames/annihilape/)
* **Udan** - Azumarill, Lopunny, Diggersby, Cinderace

## Tobal 2
