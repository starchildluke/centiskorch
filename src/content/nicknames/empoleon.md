Empoleon is a dual-type Water/Steel Pokémon, introduced in Generation 4 as the evolved form of Piplup.

Let's have a look at its origins and some of my suggested nicknames.

## Origins

Empoleon is a blue, penguin-like Pokémon with trident crown shape over its face and beak and blue edges on its wings and down its stomach.

When swimming in the water, Empoleon can move as fast as a jet boat and in Hisui it was known as the Master of the Waves.

## Nicknames

My methodology for nicknames is as follows:

* I rank nicknames by lettered tiers: S, A, B, and C. S is the best and D is the worst.
* I'll usually list my inspiration for a nickname so you know where they came from.
