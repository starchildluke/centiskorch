## Origins and data

<div class="room-box">
		<div class="room-box-left">
			<ul>
				<li><strong>Ability</strong>: Synchronize or Inner Focus (Trace for Mega Alakazam)</li>
				<li><strong>Hidden Ability</strong>: Magic Guard</li>
				<li><strong>Height</strong>: 4'11" (1.5 m)</li>
				<li><strong>Weight</strong>: 105.8 lbs. (48.0 kg)</li>
			</ul>
			<p>Alakazam was introduced in Generation 1 with a pure Psychic typing. Known as the Psi Pokémon, it's a humanoid Pokémon with mustard yellow skin and a brown torso, cuffs, and knee pads. It has three fingers on each hand and three toes on each foot, a long moustache, and it holds two spoons</p>
			<p>It also has a mega form, Mega Alakazam, introduced in Generation 6 which has a white moustache and beard, a red jewel on its forehead, and the brown parts are now longer and more purple. The two spoons are now five and levitate over its head.</p>
		</div>
