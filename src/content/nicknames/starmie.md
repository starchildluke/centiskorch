## Origins and data

<div class="room-box">
		<div class="room-box-left">
			<ul>
				<li><strong>Ability</strong>: Illuminate or Natural Cure</li>
				<li><strong>Hidden Ability</strong>: Analytic</li>
				<li><strong>Height</strong>: 3'07" or 1.1 m</li>
				<li><strong>Weight</strong>: 176.4 lbs. or 80.0 kg</li>
			</ul>
			<p>Starmie was introduced in Generation 1 with a dual Water/Psychic typing. Known as the Mysterious Pokémon, it's a purple star shaped Pokémon with a octagonal ruby in the centre of its body surrounded by a yellow octagonal frame.</p>
			<p>Starmie can now Mega Evolve into Mega Starmie using the Starminite. The mega evolution was first introduced in Pokémon Legends: Z-A. Its height is 7'07" (2.3 m) but it weighs the same.</p>
		</div>
