## Origins and data

<div class="room-box">
		<div class="room-box-left">
			<ul>
				<li><strong>Ability</strong>: Sturdy or Rock Head (Filter for Mega Aggron)</li>
				<li><strong>Hidden Ability</strong>: Heavy Metal</li>
				<li><strong>Height</strong>: 6'11" (2.1 m)</li>
				<li><strong>Weight</strong>: 793.7 lbs. (360.0 kg)</li>
			</ul>
			<p>Aggron was introduced in Generation 3 with a dual Steel/Rock typing. Known as the Iron Armor Pokémon, it's a large, bipedal Pokémon with an exoskeleton made of silver and charcoal-coloured armor. It's head looks similar to that of [Shredder from Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shredder_(Teenage_Mutant_Ninja_Turtles))</p>
			<p>It also has a mega form, Mega Aggron, introduced in Generation 6 which has lighter coloured armor and more prominent spikes on its body and head. It stands at 7'03" (2.2 m) and weighs 870.8 lbs. (395.0 kg).</p>
		</div>
