## History

Centiskorch is a Pokémon that was first introduced in Pokémon Sword & Shield in 2019. It is a Fire/Bug type and evolves from Sizzlipede starting at level 28. Centiskorch also has a Gigantamax form.

Designed by James Turner, Centiskorch is called the Radiator Pokémon and is said to be inspired by the Chinese red-headed centipede and, of course, radiators.
