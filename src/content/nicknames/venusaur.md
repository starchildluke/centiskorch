## Origins and data

<div class="room-box">
		<div class="room-box-left">

				<li><strong>Ability</strong>: Overgrow</li>
				<li><strong>Hidden Ability</strong>: Chlorophyll</li>
				<li><strong>Height</strong>: 6'07" or 2.0 m</li>
				<li><strong>Weight</strong>: 220.5 lbs. or 100.0 kg</li>
			</ul>
			<p>Venusaur was introduced in Generation 1 with a dual Grass/Poison typing. Known as the Seed Pokémon, Venusaur is greenish blue toad-like Pokémon with a big flower on its back and bumps on its legs.</p>
