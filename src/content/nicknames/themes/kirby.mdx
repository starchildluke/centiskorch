Kirby is a video game franchise published by Nintendo featuring Kirby, a pink spherical character with two arms and red shoes. Kirby has some stylistic links with Pokémon, particularly with Snorlax which was nicknamed "Kirby" by Game Freak's staff and that lead to its Japanese name, "Kabigon".

Not to mention that Kirby is a cutesy mascot like Pikachu is for Pokémon, there are lots of elemental abilities, cool levels, and crossovers with Smash Bros. The synergy is real!

## Nicknames

My methodology for picking Kirby-themed nicknames is as follows:

* I will list some games from the franchise and note down characters from each
* I will then assign Pokémon I think would match well with those characters
* It might be that some characters lend themselves well to more than one Pokémon, particular if they're based on regular animals such as fish or horses
* I will try to only mention fully evolved Pokémon (if they evolve) but if you don't plan to evolve the Pokémon in question, the name should still fit the unevolved form which I'll list
* If the name is too long and doesn't make sense after cutting letters out, I won't include it

### Kirby's Dream Land

**Characters**

* **Waddle Dee** - Mimikyu
* **Waddle Doo** - Dusclops/Dusknoir (because of the one eye)
* **Bronto Burt/Koozer** - any tiny Pokémon with wings (e.g. Route 1 birds, Cutiefly)

**Levels**

* **Green Greens** - any green Pokémon or Grass-types
* **Castle Lololo** - Palossand (because it's a sand<i>castle</i> lol)
* **Float Islands** - any Water-types
* **Bubbly Clouds** - any Flying-types and any cloud-like Pokémon like Altaria, Slurpuff, Castform, and the Genies (Tornadus, Landorus, Thundurus, and Enamorus)
* **Mt. Dedede** - any penguin-like Pokémon like Delibird, Eiscue, and Empoleon

### Kirby's Dream Land 2

* **Grass Land** - any Grass-types
* **Big Forest** - Trevenant, Tropius, Tangrowth
* **Ripple Field** - any Water-types
* **Iceberg** - any Ice-types, especially Avalugg and Regice
* **Red Canyon** - any Rock-types (especially Solrock, Crustle, Druddigon, Tyrantrum, Lycanroc
Midnight Form, Minior, and Klawf), Camerupt, Koraidon
* **Cloudy Park** - any Flying-types and any cloud-like Pokémon like Altaria, Swirlix, Castform, and the Genies (Tornadus, Landorus, Thundurus, and Enamorus)
* **Dark Castle** - shiny Palossand

### Kirby's Dream Land 3

**Characters**

* **Gooey** - Ditto, Muk, Bellibolt, Swalot
* **Rick** - any rodent Pokémon like Morpeko, Raichu, and Raticate
* **Coo** - Noctowl, Decidueye
* **Kine** - Stunfisk
* **Nago** - any cat-like Pokémon
* **Chuchu** - Octillery, Grapploct
* **Pitch** - Xatu, Squawkabilly

**Levels**

* **Grass Land** - as above
* **Ripple Field** - as above
* **Sand Canyon** - any Ground-types, especially sand-based Pokémon like Sandaconda, Sandslash, Flygon, Claydol, Garchomp, Krookodile, Hippowdon, and Palossand
* **Cloudy Park** - as above
* **Iceberg** - as above

### Kirby 64: The Crystal Shards

* **Pop Star** - Meloetta or any Fairy-types
* **Rock Star** - any Rock-types
* **Aqua Star** - any Water-types
* **Neo Star** - any Ground or Grass-types, any Pokémon that are associated with jungles, any simian Pokémon (as the game's level resembles Donkey Kong Country/Land)
* **Shiver Star** - any Ice-types
* **Ripple Star** - any Fairy-types
* **Dark Star** - any Dark-types

### Kirby: Nightmare in Dream Land

* **Veg Valley** - shortened from Vegetable Valley; Scovillain, Arboliva, Oddish, Breloom, Amoonguss, Toedscruel, Gourgeist, Sirfetch'd
* **Grape Garden** - any purple Pokémon (like Gengar and Arbok)
* **Yogurt Yard** - Alcremie, Slurpuff, any liquid Pokémon (Ditto, Muk)
* **Orange Ocean** - any orange Pokémon, especially Chi-Yu
* **RainbwResort** - shortened from Rainbow Resort; Ho-Oh, Rainbow Swirl Alcremie, Minior, Alolan Muk, Terapagos, Sigilyph, Bruxish, Blacephalon

### Kirby & The Amazing Mirror

* **RainbowRoute** - same as Rainbow Resort
* **CarrotCastle** - any orange Pokémon
* **Olive Ocean** - Arboliva
* **Radish Ruins** - Oddish, Tsareena
